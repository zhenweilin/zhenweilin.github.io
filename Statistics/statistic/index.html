Note in Advanced Statistics

click here to download full page

The note is about something that I have not learned in my undergraduate course. The courses’ main reference Statistical Inference. Thanks to my teacher Yaowu Zhang.

The note is organized as follows. In chapter6 Principals of Data Reduction, I introduce some concept about sufficient statistic, minimal sufficient statistic, ancillary statistics, complete statistics and some related theorems. In chapter7 point estimation, answer the question why MLE is a good estimator. In chapter8 hypothesis testing, introduce the concept about power function, UMP and distinguish levle $\alpha$ and size $\alpha$. A key theorem here is Neyman-Pearson Theorem.
